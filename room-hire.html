Room Hire at St Mary's

Comfortable, accessible spaces available for community groups, meetings, celebrations, and events.

Our Rooms & Facilities

A variety of spaces to suit different needs and group sizes.

👥 Fellowship Room

Our "Fellowship Room" is carpeted and has enough space for a medium sized meeting or activity. It can comfortably fit 50 people, with tables. There is also a hatch though to the kitchen for serving.

Furnishings Provided

Chairs and tables available for all events. Flexible setup options to meet your specific requirements. Range of table styles and seating to accommodate various group sizes.

🏛️ Main Hall

Our Main Hall is a sports hall sized room, and can be laid out for parties, meetings and other groups.

🔧 Office Space

Our small office space is perfect for small meetings and groups. Recently refurbished with some comfy chairs, a desk space and a soft carpet, this room feels cosy and professional at the same time!

🍳 Kitchen

The main kitchen is well-equipped to cater from for all manner of occasions and has a large oven, microwave and water boiler. There is enough crockery for a very large party. (If you are planning to cater, please note, there is a "no alcohol" policy on our premises)

Plenty of crockery

Our kitchen has enough crockery for very large parties and events. Perfect for catering occasions and providing refreshments to your guests. Ideal for celebrations and community gatherings.

🚽 Facilities

The building is fully equipped with recently refurbished accessible toilet facilities and a baby changing unit, alongside male and female toilet facilities.
