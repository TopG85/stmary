What's On at St Mary's

From Sunday worship to craft clubs and community events — there's always something happening here.

Our regular programme runs throughout the year. Everyone is welcome to join.

Groups for Every Stage of Life

Whether you're 0 or 100, there's a place for you at St Mary's.

👶 Little Stars

Tuesdays 9:30–11:30am

A relaxed and friendly group for babies, toddlers, and pre-schoolers (0–4 years) and their parents or carers. Enjoy play, songs, stories, and a warm cup of tea. A great way to meet other local families.

⛪ Junior Church

Sundays during morning service

Age-appropriate Christian education for children aged 4–11, running at the same time as the main Sunday service. Crafts, games, stories, and discussion help children explore faith in a fun, safe environment.

🎮 Young People's Group

Thursdays 6:30–8:00pm

A welcoming space for young people aged 11–18. Activities include games, discussion, creative projects, and opportunities to explore faith and life's big questions — all in a relaxed, non-pressured setting.

🧶 Craft & Chat

Mondays 2:00–4:00pm

Bring your knitting, sewing, or any craft project and enjoy friendly conversation with others. No experience necessary — complete beginners are very welcome! Tea and biscuits always provided.

📖 Bible Study Group

Wednesdays 10:00am

A midweek gathering for prayer and exploring the Bible together. The group follows structured studies and welcomes questions, doubt, and honest discussion. Beginners and experienced believers alike will feel at home.

☕ Coffee & Chat

Fridays 10:00am–12:00pm

Simply pop in for a cup of tea or coffee and a chat. No agenda, no commitment — just a friendly and welcoming space in the heart of Handforth. Perfect if you're new to the area or feeling a little lonely.

🥞 Men's Breakfast

Last Saturday of each month, 8:30am

A relaxed breakfast gathering for men of all ages. Food, fellowship, and occasionally a short discussion or speaker. A brilliant way to build friendships and start the weekend well.

🌸 Women's Fellowship

Second Tuesday of each month, 2:00pm

A warm and supportive group for women — sharing faith, life stories, seasonal activities, and outings. New members are warmly welcomed at any time of year.

🌍 Mission & Justice Group

Monthly — contact us for dates

Passionate about making the world more just and compassionate? This group supports local and global mission, fundraises for charity, and campaigns on issues of justice and care for creation.
