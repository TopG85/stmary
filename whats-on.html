What's On at St Mary's

From Sunday worship to craft clubs and community events — there's always something happening here.

What We Do

St Mary's is located at the edge of Handforth and we aim to be an active, welcoming part of our community. On these pages you can discover everything that happens regularly at our church — from weekly worship and prayer to groups for all ages and community events.

🙏 Church Open for Quiet Prayer

Our church is open on some occasions for personal prayer and quiet reflection. Check back or get in touch for current times when the church is available for prayer.

Groups & Organisations

A variety of groups meet regularly at St Mary's — something for all ages and interests.

🏠 Reflect Group

Wednesdays 7:30pm (fortnightly, September to July)

We have a regular twice-monthly House Group that meets in a house for drinks and discussion - covering all manner of topics. This is a small, friendly, informal group that don't have all the answers but like to explore the faith together. We meet on Wednesday evenings fortnightly September to July. Get in touch if you'd like to join us!

🌈 Girl Guiding — Rainbows

Not meeting at present

Part of the Girl Guiding Association. Rainbows are for the youngest members aged 4–6 years. When we resume, this group will meet in the church hall on Wednesday evenings. Check back for updates!

🎀 Girl Guiding — Brownies

Wednesdays 6:00pm–7:30pm

For girls aged 7–10 years. Girl Guides UK provides a structured programme with games, activities, and community involvement. A fantastic way for girls to make friends, build skills, and have fun. New members always welcome!

👧 Girl Guiding — Guides

Wednesdays 7:00pm–9:00pm

For girls aged 10–14 years. Guides enjoy a packed programme including games, adventure, community activities, and opportunities to get involved nationally and internationally. A vibrant, empowering group for young people.

💜 Methodist Women in Britain

Contact for dates

St Mary's has an active group of ladies involved in the Methodist Women in Britain movement — a national women's organisation that equips, encourages, and resources women. A wonderful way to connect with other women in faith and community. All welcome!
