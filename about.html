About St Mary's

A friendly, inclusive church community rooted in Handforth village for generations.

Who We Are

St Mary's is a small, friendly Methodist church with a big heart! We're situated in Handforth village, East Cheshire, and are part of the Bramhall & Wythenshawe Methodist Circuit in the North West of England District.

We are a community of people from all walks of life — young and old, long-time residents and newcomers — who have found a spiritual home together. Our congregation includes families with young children, retired individuals, professionals, and everyone in between.

At the heart of everything we do is a simple belief: that every person matters, every voice is valued, and everyone is welcome to explore faith — or simply enjoy the community — at their own pace.

Visit Us

Our Methodist Heritage

As a Methodist church, we stand in a tradition that began with John and Charles Wesley in 18th-century Britain — a tradition of vibrant worship, social action, and radical hospitality. Methodism has always been about reaching people where they are, and that continues to shape everything we do at St Mary's today.

"The world is my parish."

— John Wesley, founder of Methodism

Our Values

These principles guide how we worship, serve, and welcome one another.

🤝

Radical Welcome

We believe everyone — regardless of age, background, ability, or belief — deserves a genuine, warm welcome. We strive to be a place where no one feels like a stranger.

🙏

Authentic Faith

We encourage people to ask questions, sit with doubts, and explore faith honestly. There's no pressure — just an open invitation to discover what belief means for you.

❤️

Caring Community

We look out for one another — especially those who are vulnerable, isolated, or going through difficult times. Pastoral care and neighbourly love are central to who we are.

🌍

Service & Justice

Inspired by Jesus's call to love our neighbour, we are committed to serving our local community and advocating for a more just and compassionate world.

🎶

Joyful Worship

From traditional hymns to contemporary songs, our worship is heartfelt, accessible, and full of joy. We believe gathering together to sing and pray is a gift.

🌱

Growth & Learning

We are a community that never stops learning — through Bible study, discussion groups, prayer, and sharing life's experiences with one another.

Our Story

Decades of faithful community life in the heart of Handforth.

Founded

Roots in Handforth

St Mary's was established as a Methodist chapel serving the growing village community of Handforth, bringing together local families under one roof for worship and fellowship.

Mid-20th Century

Community Growth

As Handforth expanded, so did our congregation. The church became a hub for community life — hosting youth clubs, women's groups, and charitable activities that served the whole village.

Circuit Partnership

Bramhall & Wythenshawe Circuit

We joined the Bramhall & Wythenshawe Methodist Circuit, deepening our connections with neighbouring churches and enabling us to share resources, ministers, and mission together.

Today

A Living Community

Today, St Mary's continues to be a vibrant, welcoming presence in Handforth — adapting to the needs of our community while remaining rooted in the values that have always defined us.

  • Elected stewards who manage church life
  • Dedicated volunteers running our groups and activities

Life at St Mary's

More than just Sunday services — we're a community for every day of the week.

Families & Children

Junior Church runs most Sundays during the service, offering age-appropriate activities for children. We love having families with us!

Social Events

From summer fayres to quiz nights and shared meals, there's always something fun to bring us together as a community.

Pastoral Care

We take care of one another. Our pastoral team visits members who are unwell, isolated, or bereaved, offering support and companionship.
